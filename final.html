The Top 10 Most Underrated U.S. Vacation Destinations (per The Onion)

  1. Pittsburg, Pennsylvania
      2. If you think of Pittsburgh as an old Rust Belt city, then you haven’t been here since it was completely power-washed in the late 2000s.
  2. Indianapolis, Indiana
      3. No city offers visitors a renewed appreciation for their own hometown like Indianapolis.
  3. Cleveland, Ohio
      4. This oft-overlooked gem on the shore of Lake Erie offers the unbeatable thrill of nearly 400,000 residents insisting in unison that their city is underrated.
  4. Fort Worth, Texas
      5. Fort Worth has been working really hard on their downtown and it would really mean a lot to them if you would go pay it a visit.
  5. The Grand Canyon
      6. Wait until you hear about this place.
  6. Albany, New York
      7. All the razzle dazzle of Syracuse combined with the natural beauty of Poughkeepsie.
  7. Pierre, South Dakota
      8. Or wait, is Pierre in North Dakota? Either way, it doesn’t really matter.
  8. Madison, Wisconsin
      9. With an abundance of independent bookstores, dive bars, and hip coffeeshops, this quiet central Wisconsin city could be easily mistaken for any other college town.
  9. Clewiston, Florida
      10. Stacy lives there now.
  10. Davenport
      11. There’s a rest stop up here unless you can hold it until we get to Muscatine.
